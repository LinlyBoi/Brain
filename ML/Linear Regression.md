#ML

### AME
1/m times | ${\sum{(y-Y)}}$ |

y: Actual
Y: Prediction


### MSE
$\frac{1}{m}$ times $\sum{(Y-y)^2}$

y: Actual
Y: Prediction

### Get linear line
y = w$_1$X+w$_2$
_or_
y= (w$_1$+px)x + (w$_2$+px)
w$_1$: Slope
w$_2$: Interception

### Get Error
E= $\frac{1}{m (y-Y)^2}$
Y: w$_1$X+w$_2$
E: Error
