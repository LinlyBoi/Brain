### Rules
1. $P(z < \alpha) = \phi(\alpha)$
2. $P(z > \alpha) = 1 - \phi(\alpha)$
3. $P(\alpha_1 < z < \alpha_2) = \phi(\alpha_2) - \phi(\alpha_1)$
