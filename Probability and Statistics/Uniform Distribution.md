# Uniform Distribution
> ### Definition
>- A Random variable that is uniformly distributed over an interval [a,b]
>- Constant PDF
>- $f(x) = \frac{1}{b-a}$ where $a \leqslant x \leqslant b$
>
>### Probability
>range of values . f(x) 
>
> ### Mean
>- $E(x) =  M = \frac{a+b}{2}$
> ### Variance
>- $V(x) = \frac{{a-b}^2}{12}$
